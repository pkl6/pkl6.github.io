PETER LUK

To put it bluntly, I' am dreamer. I started in university as a business student aspired to take on the world of finance and become a highly sought after financial analyst. Fast forward to six years later, I am now on a path to become a well developed UX designer/front end developer, I have over two years of finance and marketing working experience, and have stepped foot into the startup scene. The change didn't happen over night, I had my fair shares of up and down and in those moments I've come to realize that yes, money is important but they don't define who you are. I want to be creating and being involved of something that I could eventually leave behind. I hope to one day inspired the new generations to dare to dream higher and not be afraid of getting lost in life.

In that sense, my design ethos or basically my approach to anything in general follows the simple fact that in order to achieve something great and magical, we must not be afraid to get lost. We have to get lost in our thoughts, in our imagination, creation, and ideas in order to make great work.

I'm interested in designing cool and awesome looking things as I love the to see other people loving something that I made. I hope to be able to make something that can inspire generations to come whether it's websites, comics, or films.
