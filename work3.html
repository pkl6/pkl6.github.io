Home Foods

Minimize Food Waste

interface designer. graphics designer . market analyst

OVERVIEW

The production of food waste in households is consistently a problem in the developed world. Home Foods is a mobile platform that operates as a two sided marketplace for individuals and grocery stores, which attempts to solve this issue. This report goes into greater in depth around the thinking process behind the development of Home Foods and how it is able to adequately reduce and help people manage their food waste. After a thorough investigation through piles of data, our team decided to design the site with a certain target market in mind, specifically target users include franchise grocery stores and stay at home parents, who we found were the population who frequented grocery stores most often and would perceive the greatest benefit of our innovation. I oversaw the project with four other team mates and was in charged of the entire design process from end to end. The team and I also conducted a series of surveys in order to further gather statistics for our application.

ROLE

As one of the UX / UI Designer in the team as well as the main graphics designer,

  • Started from design research and brainstorming ideas for the mobile app along with creating a survey for research
  • Wireframe sketches using Axure + high fidelity prototypes and create an interactive prototype in Adobe suites
  • Understanding the audience and conduct field research to identify users
  • Brainstorm various ideas on how to motivate users to be active on the application
  • In charge of the end-to-end graphics design process
  • Create multiple iterations of interactive prototypes
